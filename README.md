This repo records our progress for our 2026 summer project. We are tasked with building a modular simulator that emulates various types of cybersecurity threats and allows for quick testing via custom testbeds. Our goal is to test the robustness of the digital twin of the Adjuntas micro-grid and provide an easy way for laymen to run stress tests on the power systems they work with.
